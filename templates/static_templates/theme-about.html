About appliedx

appliedx digital Learning Lab is designed to advance professional education opportunities at Applied Materials. It supports technology innovation, fosters communities of practice and works to enrich the depth and range of learning experiences by leveraging digital technology to share knowledge gained in one part of the company broadly, making it more accessible to everyone. appliedx provides a seamless, integrated approach to advancing learning that will spur accelerated innovation and collaboration. appliedx Digital Learning Lab offers an array of free online courses taught by company subject matter experts to lifelong learners at Applied. Subject matter experts are provided with course development support including instructional design, video production, content development and online delivery..
